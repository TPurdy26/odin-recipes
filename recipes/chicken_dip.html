Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description:

This spicy dip is perfect for game day or any party. It's creamy, delicious, and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (15 oz) can of chicken
  • 1 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup of blue cheese crumbles
  • 1/4 cup of hot sauce (like Frank's Red Hot)
  • 1/4 cup of butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken in a skillet until fully cooked and shredded.
  2. Mix ranch dressing, blue cheese crumbles, hot sauce, and melted butter in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate pan, melt butter and whisk in flour until smooth. Gradually add milk and cook until thickened.
  4. Mix the thickened sauce with the chicken mixture.
  5. Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.
